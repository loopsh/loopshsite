Geept Our Pilot App

Geept is an innovative and versatile app, powered by the cutting-edge NLM technology. The app is designed to make users' lives more convenient and enjoyable by providing a wide range of services, all accessible through a single unified, and user-friendly platform.

01

Chatting

Geept's conversational AI capabilities enable users to engage in dynamic, interactive chats with the app. Users can ask questions, seek advice, or simply engage in friendly conversation, receiving human-like responses from the AI. This feature makes Geept an ideal personal assistant and companion, always ready to provide assistance or entertainment.

02

Web Searching

Geept's intelligent search capabilities can help users find the most relevant information from across the web. By leveraging the power of natural language processing, Geept can understand user queries and return the most accurate search results, making it a valuable tool for research or simply satisfying curiosity.

03

Image Scanning.

Geept's advanced image recognition capabilities allow users to scan images and identify objects, text, or other visual elements within them. This feature can be used to extract information, translate text, or provide details about specific objects, all in real-time.
